# Quantum Computing Basics: The Superposition Coin Spin

**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Domain:** Emerging Technologies

> *Moving beyond 1s and 0s to qubits that can exist in multiple states.*

### The Karnataka Context

Classical bits are a coin resting on heads or tails. A Qubit is the coin while it is rapidly spinning in the air—being both heads and tails simultaneously.

# Stacks: The Mysuru Pak Box

**Difficulty:** Beginner | **Domain:** Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA)

> *LIFO (Last In, First Out) principle explained through the way sweets are packed and eaten.*

### The Karnataka Context

The last piece of Mysuru Pak placed into a deep box at Sri Krishna Sweets is the first one taken out by the guest.

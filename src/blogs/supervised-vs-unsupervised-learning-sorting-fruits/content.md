# Supervised vs Unsupervised Learning: Sorting Fruits

**Difficulty:** Intermediate | **Domain:** Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

> *The difference between training with labeled data versus letting the algorithm find patterns.*

### The Karnataka Context

Supervised: Showing an AI 100 labeled photos of Alphonso vs. Totapuri mangoes. Unsupervised: Giving it a massive basket of mixed local fruits and asking it to group similar ones.

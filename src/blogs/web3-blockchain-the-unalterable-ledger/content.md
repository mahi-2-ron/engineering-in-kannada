# Web3 & Blockchain: The Unalterable Ledger ⛓️💰

**Difficulty:** Intermediate | **Domain:** Emerging Technologies

> "Traditional trust is a central pillar; Web3 trust is a shared network."

### The Village Record-Book (ಕಟ್ಟೆ ಪಂಚಾಯಿತಿ ಲೆಡ್ಜರ್)

Imagine a small village in **Mandya**. Everyone knows everyone.
- **The Old Way (Centralized)**: The village has one accountant (**Shanbhoga**). He has a big book. If **Ramu** gives 100 Rs to **Somu**, the accountant writes it down. If the accountant is corrupt or someone burns his house, the record is gone!
- **The Web3 Way (Decentralized)**: Every single person in the village has their own copy of the record-book. 
    - When Ramu gives 100 Rs to Somu, he stands in the village center (the **Decentralized Network**) and shouts, *"I gave 100 Rs to Somu!"*
    - Everyone takes out their book and writes it down.
    - If Somu later tries to lie and says, *"No, he gave me only 50 Rs,"* everyone else looks at their books and says, *"No, we all wrote 100. You are wrong!"*

This is **Blockchain**. It's a chain of digital "blocks" where information is stored such that it's impossible to change, hack, or cheat the system.

## 1. What is Web3? (ನಮ್ಮ ಭಾಷೆಯಲ್ಲಿ ಸಾಫ್ಟ್ ವೇರ್!)

- **Web 1.0**: You can only read (like a newspaper).
- **Web 2.0**: You can read and write (like Facebook/Instagram). But Mark Zuckerberg owns your data.
- **Web 3.0**: You read, you write, and **you OWN**. The data is on a blockchain, not on a single company's server.

**ಸರಳವಾಗಿ ಹೇಳೋದಾದ್ರೆ:** ವೆಬ್ 3.0 ಅಂದ್ರೆ 'ಇಂಟರ್ನೆಟ್ ನ ಜನರಿಗಾಗಿ, ಜನರಿಂದಲೇ ನಡೆಸಲ್ಪಡುವ ಯುಗ'. ಇಲ್ಲಿ ಯಾವುದೋ ಒಂದು ದೊಡ್ಡ ಕಂಪನಿಯ ಮಾತು ನಡೆಯಲ್ಲ, ಬದಲಾಗಿ ಇಡೀ ನೆಟ್ ವರ್ಕ್ ನ ಮಾತು ನಡೆಯುತ್ತೆ.

---

## 2. Why is it "Unalterable"? (ಯಾಕೆ ಇದನ್ನ ಚೇಂಜ್ ಮಾಡೋಕೆ ಆಗಲ್ಲ?)

Once a block is added to the blockchain, it's linked to the previous block using a secret mathematical code called a **Hash**. If you try to change one number in an old block:
1. The hash changes.
2. The next block's link breaks.
3. Every block after that breaks!

To cheat, you'd have to hack 51% of all the computers in the world at the same time. **ಇದು ಅಸಾಧ್ಯ!**

## 3. Real-World Applications

- **Cryptocurrency**: Digital money like Bitcoin that no government can stop.
- **NFTs**: Digital ownership of art or property.
- **Smart Contracts**: Auto-executing contracts (like a vending machine that gives you a soda only when you put the coin).

**ನಮ್ಮ ತ್ಯಾಪೆ:** ಇದನ್ನ ನೀವು ಯಾವುದೋ ಒಂದು ಸೈಟ್ ಅಥವಾ ಜಮೀನಿನ ಪಹಣಿ (RTC) ಗೆ ಹೋಲಿಸಬಹುದು. ಯಾರೋ ಒಬ್ಬರು ತಿದ್ದಲು ಆಗದ ಹಾಗೆ ಪಕ್ಕಾ ಸಿಸ್ಟಮ್ ಮಾಡಿದ್ರೆ ಎಷ್ಟು ಚೆನ್ನಾಗಿರುತ್ತೆ ಅಲ್ವಾ? ಅದೇ ಬ್ಲಾಕ್ ಚೈನ್!

## 4. Engineering Pro-Tip: Gas Fees

Nothing is free! Running all these validation checks on thousands of computers costs electricity and computing power. To pay for this, we use **Gas Fees**. It's like paying a small commission to the villagers for writing in their books. If the network is busy, the gas fees go up!

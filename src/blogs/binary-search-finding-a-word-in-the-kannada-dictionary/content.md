# Binary Search: Finding a Word in the Kannada Dictionary

**Difficulty:** Beginner | **Domain:** Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA)

> *The power of O(log n) time complexity by dividing the search space in half.*

### The Karnataka Context

Finding the meaning of "Tantragnyana" (Technology) in a physical Kannada dictionary by opening the middle and deciding left or right.

# Augmented Reality (AR) vs Virtual Reality (VR)

**Difficulty:** Beginner | **Domain:** Emerging Technologies

> *The difference between overlaying digital elements on the real world versus full immersion.*

### The Karnataka Context

VR is putting on a headset to virtually walk through the Mysore Palace. AR is pointing your phone camera at a street in Church Street and seeing floating reviews above restaurants.

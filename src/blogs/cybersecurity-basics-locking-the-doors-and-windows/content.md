# Cybersecurity Basics: Locking the Doors and Windows

**Difficulty:** Beginner | **Domain:** Cybersecurity

> *The core triad of security (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability) explained simply.*

### The Karnataka Context

Imagine your house in Davangere. You lock your main door to keep secrets (Confidentiality), you ensure no one can tamper with your certificates (Integrity), and you always have a spare key to enter (Availability). This is exactly how cybersecurity works for your data.

---

## 1. The CIA Triad (ಭದ್ರತೆಯ ಮೂರು ಸ್ತಂಭಗಳು)

In cybersecurity, everything revolves around these three concepts:

1. **Confidentiality (ರಹಸ್ಯವಾಗಿಡುವುದು)**: Ensuring that sensitive data is only accessed by authorized people. (ಉದಾಹರಣೆಗೆ, ನಿಮ್ಮ ಮೊಬೈಲ್ ಪಾಸ್‌ವರ್ಡ್ ಬೇರೆಯವರಿಗೆ ಗೊತ್ತಾಗಬಾರದು.)
2. **Integrity (ದೋಷರಹಿತವಾಗಿಡುವುದು)**: Ensuring that data is accurate and has not been tampered with. (ಉದಾಹರಣೆಗೆ, ನಿಮ್ಮ ಬ್ಯಾಂಕ್ ಅಕೌಂಟ್‌ನಲ್ಲಿರುವ ಮೊತ್ತ ಯಾರೂ ನಿಮ್ಮ ಅನುಮತಿಯಿಲ್ಲದೆ ಬದಲಾಯಿಸಬಾರದು.)
3. **Availability (ಲಭ್ಯವಿರುವುದು)**: Ensuring that data and systems are accessible when needed. (ಉದಾಹರಣೆಗೆ, ನಿಮಗೆ ಬೇಕಾದಾಗ ಇಂಟರ್ನೆಟ್ ಬ್ಯಾಂಕಿಂಗ್ ಕೆಲಸ ಮಾಡಲೇಬೇಕು.)

---

## 2. Common Digital Threats

- **Phishing**: Fake emails or SMS (like the fake "Electricity Bill" messages) designed to steal your pins. (ನಕಲಿ ಮೆಸೇಜ್‌ಗಳನ್ನು ಕಳುಹಿಸಿ ನಿಮ್ಮ ಹಣ ಕದಿಯುವ ತಂತ್ರ.)
- **Malware**: Harmful software that can spy on you or lock your files.
- **Data Breaches**: When big companies lose your private data to hackers.

---

## 3. How to Protect Yourself? (ನಮ್ಮನ್ನು ನಾವು ರಕ್ಷಿಸಿಕೊಳ್ಳುವುದು ಹೇಗೆ?)

- **Use Strong Passwords**: Don't use your name or phone number.
- **Enable 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication)**: Like the OTP you get for transactions. (OTP ಬಳಸಿ ಇನ್ನೂ ಹೆಚ್ಚಿನ ಭದ್ರತೆ ಪಡೆಯಿರಿ.)
- **Keep Software Updated**: Those "System Update" notifications are actually security fixes.

# Latency vs Bandwidth: Water Pipes from KRS Dam

**Difficulty:** Intermediate | **Domain:** Computer Networks (CN)

> *The difference between how fast data travels versus how much data can travel at once.*

### The Karnataka Context

Latency is how fast the water physically flows from KRS Dam to Bangalore. Bandwidth is the width of the Cauvery water pipe.

# Internet of Things (IoT): The Smart Farm in Mandya

**Difficulty:** Beginner | **Domain:** Emerging Technologies

> *Connecting physical sensors to the internet for data-driven actions.*

### The Karnataka Context

Soil moisture sensors in a sugarcane field in Mandya that automatically text the farmer and turn on the water pumps when the mud is too dry.
